about us

what makes our Pickle special?

A homegrown brand born out of the sheer love and longing for grandma’s culinary masterpieces, we aim to recreate the magic of delicious homemade pickles. Started by a food enthusiast who was always fascinated by the unique pickle recipes that were carved out by her grand mother, we use fresh handpicked ingredients to create pickles which taste just like the good, old homemade pickles.

The pickles are prepared in small batches with utmost care and grandma’s love. We believe in using no artificial preservatives and ensuring that our patrons receive the best is our priority.
